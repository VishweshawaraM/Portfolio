Curriculum Frameworks & Research

Pedagogy Visual Grammar

Developing visual mapping techniques to explain complex Sanskrit verb conjugations (Tiṅanta) and case endings (Subanta). This system bridges the gap between rote memorization and logical understanding for non-native speakers.

Research Vedic Phonetics

A structured progression system for teaching Taittirīya Upaniṣad. Focusing on the breakdown of Sandhi rules within Mantras to ensure learners maintain Svara integrity during recitation.
