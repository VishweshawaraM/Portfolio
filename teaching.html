Teaching Domains

Sanskrit Language

Grammar Literature

A structured approach to the divine language, moving from basic script recognition to complex sentence construction and Sandhi rules.

  • Devanāgarī Script: Writing and pronunciation mechanics.
  • Kāraka & Vibhakti: Understanding the case system.
  • Sandhi & Samāsa: The science of word conjunctions.
  • Subhāṣita Analysis: Applying grammar to literature.

Bhagavad Gītā

Philosophy Chanting

The Gītā is not merely a book to be read, but a manual for life. We study it verse by verse, paying attention to the commentary tradition.

  • Chapter-wise progression.
  • Chanting with proper meter (Chandas).
  • Alignment with Śaṅkara Bhāṣya (Commentary).
  • Philosophical definitions of Karma, Jñāna, and Bhakti.

Krishna Yajurveda

Veda Svara

The Vedas are preserved through sound. This stream focuses exclusively on the auditory discipline required to recite Mantras.

  • Svara (Intonation) Discipline (Udātta, Anudātta, Svarita).
  • Mantra Pushpam & Rudram.
  • Sūktas (Puruṣa, Nārāyaṇa, Durga).
  • Oral transmission method strictly followed.
