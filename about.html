The Gurukula Tradition

[span_0](start_span)THE FOUNDATION[span_0](end_span)

7 Years of Immersion

My education was not a mere accumulation of degrees, but a transformation of lifestyle. Living in a traditional Gurukula (Veda Vignana Gurukulam), I studied Vyākaraṇa (Grammar), Sāhitya (Literature), and Advaita Vedānta under the strict discipline of the lineage.

[span_1](start_span)THE DISCIPLINE[span_1](end_span)

Pronunciation & Integrity

In an era of simplified learning, I uphold the necessity of Śikṣā (Phonetics). The Veda is preserved through sound. My teaching emphasizes the exactness of *Svara* (intonation) and the fidelity to the original texts, avoiding diluted interpretations.

[span_2](start_span)THE MISSION[span_2](end_span)

Bhāṣya-Centric Pedagogy

We study the Bhagavad Gītā and Upaniṣads not through modern opinions, but through the authorized commentaries (Bhāṣya) of Ādi Śaṅkarācārya. This ensures the student receives the knowledge exactly as it was intended by the Ṛṣis.
