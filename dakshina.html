Understanding Dakṣiṇā

In the Gurukula tradition, the exchange between teacher and student is not merely a commercial transaction. It is an offering of gratitude (Śraddhā) that sustains the lineage.

While we live in a modern economic structure, I strive to maintain the sanctity of this relationship. The contributions for classes are viewed as Guru Dakṣiṇā—an offering that enables the continuation of this teaching work.
